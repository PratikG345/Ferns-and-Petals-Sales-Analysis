# 🌸 Ferns and Petals Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard)
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales performance of Ferns and Petals (FNP), an online gifting platform specializing in occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

The dashboard was built in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, and Slicers to answer business questions and provide actionable insights.

## 🔍 Key Insights
### Revenue
- Total Revenue Generated in Rs. 35.2 lakhs.
- Maximum Revenue was generated on Anniversaries and Rakshabandhan.
- Maximum Revenue peaked during August, February and March.
- Lowest sales were recorded in month of January followed my July and September.
- Tuesdays and Sundays are the highest revenue-generating days of the week, with both days crossing the ₹6,00,000 mark.
### Orders
- Total Orders Generated in the Year 2023 was 1000.
- Imphal City in Manipur generated the maximum orders.
### Delivery & Spending
- Average Order-Delivery Time stands at 5.53 days across all orders.
- Average Customer Spending (Average Order Value) is ₹3,520.98 per transaction.
### Products
- The "Magnam Set" is the number one product by revenue, crossing the ₹1,00,000 threshold.
- The remaining top 5 revenue-generating products are Quia Gift, Dolores Gift, Harum Pack, and Deserunt Box, all maintaining a highly competitive and close revenue distribution.
